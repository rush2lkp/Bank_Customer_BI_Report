# Customer Banking Behavior and KPI Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer demographics, financial behavior, and banking KPIs to generate actionable insights for business decision-making.

The goal is to help the bank:
- Understand customer behavior patterns  
- Identify high-value customer segments  
- Track financial performance  
- Improve marketing and customer engagement strategies  

---

## Objectives

### Customer Behavior Insights
- Analyze customer behavior based on:
  - Marital Status  
  - Age Group  
  - Job Classification  
- Identify high-value customer segments for targeted campaigns  

---

### KPI Dashboard Development
Develop interactive dashboards to track:
- Average Bank Balance  
- Total Deposits  
- Customer Growth Trends  

---

### Financial Profiling and Segmentation
- Segment customers into:
  - High-balance vs Low-balance  
  - Income or Job-based segments  
- Provide personalized banking product recommendations  

---

### Geographical Market Analysis
- Analyze customer distribution across cities or regions  
- Identify high-performing regions  
- Recommend strategies to replicate success in other areas  

---

### Gender-Based Insights
- Compare financial behavior between male and female customers  
- Identify gaps in engagement  
- Suggest strategies to attract and retain female customers  

---

## Problem Statements

### Customer Demographics and Behavior
How do demographic factors such as:
- Marital status  
- Age group  
- Job classification  
- Gender  

influence customer banking behavior and financial trends?

---

### KPI Performance Analysis
How effectively is the bank achieving its KPIs related to:
- Customer balances  
- Financial growth  

---

### Customer Segmentation
How can customers be segmented based on:
- Financial profiles  
- Job types  
- Geographic locations  

to identify high-value segments?

---

### Regional Market Analysis
- Why do certain regions have the highest number of customers?  
- How can this trend be leveraged for expansion?

---

### Gender-Based Financial Trends
- What drives the difference in behavior between male and female customers?  
- How can the bank improve engagement with female customers?
